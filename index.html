The Wizard Code

1.

There is no law above a Wizard's will.

2.

A Wizard will not enlighten those who do not ask and be wary of those that do ask.

3.

A Wizard is liberated from material addictions but carry tresaure always.

4.

A Wizard will wield the pen and sword as equal instruments.

5.

A Wizard knows there is no escaping a Wizard's duel.

6.

A Wizard is neither good nor evil.

7.

A Wizard will only master one school of magic but may be profient in all schools.

8.

A Wizard's intuition is never wrong.

9.

A Wizard understands the Wizard walk.

10.

A Wizard will use no more energy than is required.
